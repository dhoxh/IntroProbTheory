Introduction to Probability Theory

---

## Course Overview
An introduction to the theory of probability. Topics include counting, conditional probability and independent events, discrete and continuous random variables and their distributions, representation, moment generating functions, multivariate distributions, functions of variables, transformations, sampling distributions, and the central limit theorem. Students will demonstrate understanding of these concepts and apply them to practical problems.
